LO-FI APERITIFS

VERMOUTHS & AMARO

Lo-Fi Aperitifs are a line of vermouths and amaro crafted with California wines and natural herbs flavors & botanicals. Our goal was to create a liquid that can be enjoyed on its own, or used generously in a cocktail — not just whispered over a martini.

Lo-Fi provides both home bartenders and mixologists with the perfect tools for creating delicious cocktails, whether they’re using classic 1:1 proportions or mixing up new creations. We infuse our wine with a unique blend of botanicals that are listed right on the label.

Enjoy them mixed in your favorite cocktail or sipped on their own.
