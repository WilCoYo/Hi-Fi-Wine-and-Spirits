OUR COCKTAILS

MANHATTAN

Lo-Fi Sweet Vermouth

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 oz Lo-Fi Sweet Vermouth
  • 1 1/2 ox rye whiskey
  • 2 dashes bitters
  • Orange peel

Instructions

Combine ingredients in a cocktail pitcher and stir with ice. Strain into a coupe glass and garnish with orange peel and/or brandied cherry.

MARTINI

Lo-Fi Dry Vermouth

Ingredients

  • 1.5 oz Lo-Fi Dry Vermouth
  • 1.5 oz London dry Style Gin
  • 2 Dashes Orange Bitters
  • Lemon Peel

Instructions

Combine ingredients in a cocktail pitcher and stir with ice. Pour into a coupe or martini glass and garnish with lemon peel.
